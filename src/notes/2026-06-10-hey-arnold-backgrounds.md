<p>I didn't watch a whole lot of this series when it aired in the 1990s, but clearly the artists had a lot of fun designing the backgrounds (city streets, apartment interiors) and filling them with fun details.</p>
<p>Looking at these drawings, I can't help but think of <a href="https://theoatmeal.com/comics/ai_art">A Cartoonist's Review of AI Art</a>, a recent webcomic I saw which lamented the decline of jobs creating backgrounds for animation art, among other things.</p>
