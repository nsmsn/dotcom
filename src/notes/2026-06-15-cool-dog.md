Here's a sticker I had affixed to a plastic container we would keep dog food in. I bought it in a local shop in Providence. Years later, I met an artist with this same sticker on her water bottle in Santa Fe. She attended RISD and was friends with [Mickey Z](https://mickeyz.org/) the artist behind [this particular sticker](https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1668326929/cool-dog-annual-sticker-fund-2016).
