Here's a sticker I slapped on a container we would keep dog food in. I found the sticket at a local shop in Providence, RI, while we were there for an [art show](https://grinprovidence.squarespace.com/news/interview-barbara-weissberger-eleanor-aldrich-and-charlie-smith) Eleanor was featured in. 

Years later, I met an artist in Santa Fe with this same sticker on her water bottle. She attended RISD, lived in Providence and was friends with [Mickey Z](https://mickeyz.org/), the artist behind [this particular sticker](https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1668326929/cool-dog-annual-sticker-fund-2016).
