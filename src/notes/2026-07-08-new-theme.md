Fun things can happen when you name your stylesheet <code>interim.css</code>. It didn't take me long to tire of pure black and white, and I've always wanted to use eggplant as a dark mode background color. I used the [Meyerweb Color Blender tool](https://meyerweb.com/eric/tools/color-blend/#F6EFF6:2C1F2C:10:hex) to mix a little lavender into a "French porcelain" white color and then create some color steps between the lightest and darkest background/text colors. I'm using some brighter candy shades for hover effects. 

The screenshot above is from [Equinox](https://equinox.netigen.com/), a handy tool that lets you view light mode and dark mode at the same time.
