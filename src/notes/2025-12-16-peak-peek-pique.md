[**Peak**](https://www.dictionary.com/browse/peak): The highest point or level; the maximum point, degree, or volume of anything.<br>
_The peak of her political career._

[**Peek**](https://www.dictionary.com/browse/peek): to look or glance quickly or furtively, especially through a small opening or from a concealed location; a quick or furtive look or glance.<br>
_Take a peek at their website for other ideas and inspiration._

[**Pique**](https://www.dictionary.com/browse/pique): to excite (interest, curiosity, etc.). To arouse an emotion or provoke to action.<br>
_This should pique your interest._
