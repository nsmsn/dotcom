I enjoyed [Rob Weychert's writeup on building his listening diary](https://v7.robweychert.com/blog/2026/07/v7-listening-diary/), from Last.fm data going back to 2005.

> I’m glad to have it. Some entries are documents of how I self-soothed during a challenging time. Many make space for artists I kept coming back to who didn’t previously have a presence on the site, often because I streamed their music without writing about them or buying any of their records or seeing them live. Hopefully going back through the listening diary will help me start to rectify that.
