Check out [Ron's Musical Retrospective](https://listening.ronbronson.com/) detailing 21 years of listening data from Last.fm. His page is based on a toolkit from [Joel's Listening Autobiography](https://joelgoodman.co/timeline/) (also 21 years). 

I love the storytelling aspect to these pages, combining a written narrative with compeliing interactive data visualizations, as well as audio previews. From the credits it looks like these pages are made with the Last.fm API for music data, the Discogs API for artwork, D3.js for dataviz stuff, and Apple Music is providing the audio previews.
